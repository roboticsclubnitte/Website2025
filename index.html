About Us

Established in 2014 at NMAMIT, the Robotics Club is an interdisciplinary club with a motive to provide practical, hands-on experience in various applications of robotics. As the official robotics and multidisciplinary club of the institute, it gives students of various disciplines exposure to work on complex projects. The club focuses on designing, building, and creating various mechanical and electronic bots, regularly conducts workshops on Bot building, Internet of Things, 3D modelling, and Programming languages, and actively participates in various technical fests and events at national and international levels.

Our Divisions

Combat Robotics

Our combat robotics division is involved in building various bots like Race bots, Soccer bots, sumo bots and War bots. We focus on design, testing, fabrication, and control.

Soft Robotics

We explore the field of soft robotics by designing, creating, testing, and implementing line followers, maze solvers, and various IoT hobby projects.

Aero Division

Our Aero division involves the design, fabrication, and implementation of drones and rockets.

Recent Events & Workshops
